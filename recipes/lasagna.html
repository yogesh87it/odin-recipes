Lasanga

In this classic lasagna recipe, sheets of pasta are layered with a cheesy filling, a rich meaty tomato sauce, and more cheese and then baked until bubbly and browned

Ingredients

  • ricotta and parmesan with seasonings
  • Italian sausage
  • pasta sauce
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles
  2. Cook sausage with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
